Red Zone Anarchy region

Since the outbreak this region is tormented by pain, terror and death. Hostile groups of bandits, wild animals, zombie hordes are seen here regularly. Entering this zone will bring you nothing but trouble and misery. If you still want to enter this area, be well prepared and prepare to die.

Despite everything, there is a strong suspicion that valuable goods are regularly found in this area. The area is largely undiscovered and could be a excellent scavenger hunt.

Blue Zone Signs of civilization

After years of chaos, murder and destruction new hope arises in this region.Survivors have started to work together here and are trying to form a "stable" region in Chernarus.The environment is still hostile but the people here are mostly friendly and trying to build their lives.As it seems that this region will find its way back to normal life by showing the first Signs of Civilization there are still bad things happening here.

Survivors aren't completely safe here as raiding, robbery and terrorizing are still taking place by bandits that are seeking for wealth.

Signs of civilization (blue zone) is a "No-KOS" region. This means you are not allowed to attack players on sight. We call this "semi-roleplay". But that doesn't make this region peaceful, bandits are allowed to take you hostage or rob you.

Freelance Trader

  • Player driven trader.
  • Gear, guns, food, meds, special goods.
  • Sells unique items

A mercenary trader group that scavenges for all kinds of goods, made of different people from all around the world that has established 2 main trading points throughout the area of Chernarus. Focused on establishing a stable economy and a friendly enviroment whilst maintaining a neutral reputation amongst other factions, specialised in trading Guns, Meds, Food, Special goods and some Unique exotic items.

Toxic Zones

  • Outbreak
  • Toxic cloud
  • Left over toxic military gear
  • Chernobyl

As an aftermath of the Outbreak in 2012-2013 in the areas of Nuclear Powerplants and some failed Military experiments which were performed in hopes of saving the humanity were created so called "Toxic Zones". Those so called Toxic zones contain remnants of Military bases filled with wildlife and zombies, some say there is crates that are marked with "Toxic Chest", its said that they contain some high end Military gear of high value. However you require some special clothing to go inside of such zones, they're all filled with toxic gases that melt your skin away, many brave men have tried going inside of it, however they weren't seen ever since.

Mining

  • Ores
  • Uses for ores
  • Use in survival
  • Regional mining

Chernarus is a land rich with ores which were important for the industry, although after the aftermath they dont have the same use as before, Gold and Silver is used for trading purposes, Charcoal and Sulfur are used to create gunpowder which has numerous utilizations, mainly used in breaching obstacles and resolving conflicts. With all of the chaos around, in the southern areas you can still find friendly miners that work hard for their money, however in the Northern areas the story is a little bit different...

Helicopter Crashes

  • RNG gear
  • Outbreak
  • Panic
  • crashes
  • wolves and zombies

In the panic of the outbreak civilians and the military had their differences, sadly they were settled with agression which resulted in numerous victims. However in this current state such events can produce a good source of weaponry and equipment, once a civilian militia takes down a Helicopter everyone waits for the fire to settle down and then storm towards it in hopes of finding high end Military equipment which could be resold at the local Freelance Trading post. Besides Helicopters the militia will attack Police vehicles that transport more civilian weaponry and at rare occasions storage containers as well.

Drug Dealer

  • Drugs :)

After the outbreak when the dust settled people seeked a remedy of some kind, so a few survivors saw a chance to make some good profit in others despair so they have opened a drug dealing buisness in the North-West part of Chernarus. Even though its in the most dangerous part of Chernarus people still go there in order to sell their goods or to purchase them, but beware of wildlife in that part of Chernarus, there is high amounts of bears and wolves, for your safety it is suggested to go heavily armed.
